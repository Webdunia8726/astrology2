Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about astrology.

Astrology is the study of celestial bodies' positions and movements, and how they influence human affairs and natural phenomena.
Astrology can provide insights into your personality, relationships, and life path, helping you make informed decisions.
Astrology can offer predictions based on your natal chart, but it is essential to remember that it guides rather than dictates your future.
There are several branches of astrology, including Natal Astrology, Horary Astrology, Electional Astrology, and Mundane Astrology, each serving different purposes.
The accuracy of astrology depends on the skill of the astrologer and the specifics of the chart. Many find it to be a helpful tool for self-reflection and guidance.
